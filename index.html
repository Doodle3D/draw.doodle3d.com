Your computer is currently not connected to the Doodle3D WiFiBox (the page you see now comes from our website).

You can use the WiFiBox in two ways: As standalone AccessPoint and as a Client in your existing network:

In Accesspoint-mode the Doodle3D WiFiBox creates its own network (something like Doodle3D-AB1234). This is the default behavior of the box. You can connect to the network the way you would connect to any other network from your tablet, laptop or smartphone. When connected to the network please reload this page.

In Client-mode the Doodle3D WiFiBox connects to an existing wireless network (for example your home-network). You can connect to your box using this address: connect.doodle3d.com.
